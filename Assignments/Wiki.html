Build Computers

On top of gaming at some point, I wanted to see if I could make my games look better than they currently did as well as run smoother which led me to Building my own computer. There was already a slight interest in computers because my Father did at one point own his own company revolving around computer, but it wasn't coding based nor was it the internal parts of a computer. He worked more with POS systems and Server based problems and after tagging along on a few trips it did peak my interest in computers but not in the same field. After looking up NUMEROUS tutorials and websites like MicroCenter to see the best parts and comparisons between each one I've managed to build two computers that work relatively smoothly for both me and my mother, and Although I've built two computers by no means am I an expert, but it does feel great to be able to push the power button and watch everything come to life after hooking all of the wires together and to see the smoothness of frames in the games I play on my computer.
